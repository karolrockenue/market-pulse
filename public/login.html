Frequently Asked Questions

Is my hotel's data secure?

Absolutely. Your data is isolated and never shared individually. All competitor data is aggregated and anonymized, so your specific performance metrics are only visible to you.

Does this app make changes in my PMS?

No. Market Pulse has read-only access. Our application only reads the data required for analytics and has no permission to create, edit, or delete any information in your property management system.

How is "The Market" calculated?

The market is a competitive set of hotels with a similar quality tier (star rating) to your own. This ensures you're comparing your performance against true, like-for-like competitors.

How often is the data refreshed?

Data is automatically synced from your PMS every 24 hours, typically overnight, to ensure you have the freshest insights each morning.

Why is the platform free? What's the catch?

There's no catch. Market Pulse is currently offered completely free as we build our user base and gather feedback. Our goal is to provide immense value to hoteliers. In the future, we may introduce optional premium features, but the core dashboard will remain free for early adopters.

How do I add another hotel to my account?

To connect additional properties, you must initiate the connection from within the PMS account for that specific property. Find Market Pulse in your PMS Marketplace for the new hotel and follow the connection steps. Once connected, it will automatically appear in your property switcher on the dashboard.

How do I disconnect the app?

You can revoke access at any time directly from the "Connected Apps" or "Marketplace" section within your PMS settings. This will immediately stop any future data synchronization.

Can I request a new PMS integration?

Yes! We are always looking to expand our list of supported PMS providers. Please send your integration requests to our support email.

Where can I send suggestions for new features?

We would love to hear your ideas! Please send all feedback and feature suggestions to our support email, which can be found in the "Contact" section.
