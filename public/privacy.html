Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Market Pulse, operated by ROCKENUE INTERNATIONAL GROUP ("we", "us", or "our"), collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our services. Our registered address is 5842+632 Grand Stand, Nad Al Sheba 1, Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

By using the Market Pulse service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

a) Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify you ("Personal Data"). This includes: First name and last name, and Email address.

b) Hotel Performance Data

When you connect your Property Management System (PMS) to our Service, we access specific hotel performance data on a read-only basis. We do not have permission to create, edit, or delete any information within your PMS. This data includes, but is not limited to: Aggregated performance metrics (e.g., Occupancy, ADR, RevPAR), and General property details (e.g., property name, city, quality tier).

c) Usage Data and Cookies

We collect information on how the Service is accessed and used ("Usage Data"). This may include information such as your computer's IP address, browser type, and browser version. We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track the activity on our Service. We use third-party services like Google Analytics to monitor and analyze the use of our Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

Market Pulse uses the collected data to provide and improve our Service, create aggregated and anonymized market data for benchmarking, manage your account, and monitor service usage for improvements.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or rent your Personal Data or individual Hotel Performance Data. We may disclose information to third-party service providers like Vercel (hosting), Neon (database), SendGrid (email), and Google Analytics, who are obligated to protect your data.

4. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data for as long as your account is active, and will delete it within 1 year of account termination. Anonymized and aggregated hotel performance data is retained indefinitely for market analysis.

5. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, update, delete, or object to the processing of your Personal Data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us by email at: support@market-pulse.io
